Title: Bio

----

Headline: UX/UI designer.

----

Text: 

You know what's at the heart of *humilitarian* design? Empathy for the needs of all people. Designers must support a person's physical, emotional, and mental abilities. Dan Gilmore helps create interfaces that put people first. His design methodology is simple: Research empowers insight, and insight empowers empathy.  
 
The translation of user experience (UX) design into code is important to Dan. He's been designing web experiences for more than 10 years. He is passionate about accessibility, semantic HTML, and progressive enhancement. It boils down to one simple outcome. When we support all people who use our digital products, we make life better for all people.
