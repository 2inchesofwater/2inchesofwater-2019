Description: 

Musta connects fitness trainers with new leads. 

Musta is a unique fitness trainer payment and organisation system, a potential lead generator, and business companion for fitness training professionals.

The site is built on Magento, with hand-crafted mobile-first SASS CSS styling on a custom framework derived from Bootstrap.

UX and UI concepts by Dan Gilmore for iFactory.
